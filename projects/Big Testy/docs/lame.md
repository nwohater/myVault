# PRD: TaskFlow — Smart Task Manager

## Overview

TaskFlow is a mobile-first task management app designed for individuals and small teams who want a lightweight, intuitive way to organize their work without the bloat of enterprise tools.

---

## Problem Statement

Most task managers are either too simple (no structure) or too complex (steep learning curve). TaskFlow fills the gap for users who want just enough structure to stay productive without feeling like they're managing their task manager.

---

## Goals

- Help users capture, organize, and complete tasks quickly
- Reduce friction in daily planning
- Provide lightweight collaboration for small teams (2–5 people)

---

## Target Users

- **Primary:** Freelancers and solo professionals
- **Secondary:** Small teams and startups

---

## Key Features

### 1. Quick Capture
- Add a task in under 3 seconds from anywhere in the app
- Support for natural language input (e.g., "Call client tomorrow at 3pm")

### 2. Smart Prioritization
- Auto-suggest priority based on due date and past behavior
- Daily "Focus List" — a curated view of the 3–5 most important tasks for today

### 3. Projects & Tags
- Group tasks into projects
- Tag tasks for cross-project filtering

### 4. Team Collaboration
- Assign tasks to teammates
- Comment threads on tasks
- Shared project views

### 5. Integrations
- Calendar sync (Google, Apple)
- Slack notifications
- Email-to-task via forwarding address

---

## Out of Scope (v1)

- Time tracking
- Gantt charts / timeline views
- Enterprise SSO / admin controls
